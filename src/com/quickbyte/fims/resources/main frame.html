QuickByte Student Profiling System

this system made by a group of students studying in Centro Escolar University legazpi village campus, for Makati Medical Center College, The student profiling system will decrease the workload of the guidance department and the psychology department. because just one click they can see who's enrolled in their school. And also the search part is filtered, you can search by name, student number, birth rank, nationality, civil status, and etc. Also they can schedule sessions for the students by just clicking, no need to write it in a log book because the system itself will do it for them and the guidance counselor can type his/her findings about the students in the system. and with just one click you can also print the background of the student.
